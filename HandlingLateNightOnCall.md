# Purpose
This document provides the steps to take when on call and how to handle "Deep Sleep" issues

# Process
## Take your phone off of "Do Not Disturb"
![DoNotDisturb](https://github.com/mahldcat/GeneralDocs/assets/1320535/72373f06-69c8-406f-92c0-6c1d85c578ae)
## Set your Ringer Volume to 100%
![RingerVolume](https://github.com/mahldcat/GeneralDocs/assets/1320535/481c3c8f-70b0-4fe4-823e-f8c5d0b3af68)
## Put your device on charge next to your bed
![ChargePhone](https://github.com/mahldcat/GeneralDocs/assets/1320535/5ccd89ad-cc02-41f0-a713-bfd428020355)
## When the phone rings, sit up and respond
![Respond](https://github.com/mahldcat/GeneralDocs/assets/1320535/2cb21bde-cee4-45ea-a75a-30741c710656)
## Go to your laptop and login
![LoginAndWork](https://github.com/mahldcat/GeneralDocs/assets/1320535/34c2f289-565f-461e-b553-e6e9b745430e)
## Do NOT turn off the phone and go back to sleep
![donotsleep](https://github.com/mahldcat/GeneralDocs/assets/1320535/15bcde49-ce3a-4140-8080-db1e91a6fba5)
